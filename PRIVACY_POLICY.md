# Privacy Policy for Cents and Sense

**Last Updated: March 6, 2026**

## Introduction

Cents and Sense ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal and financial information.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Collect

Cents and Sense collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Personal Information**: Your name and profile preferences
- **Financial Data**:
  - Account information (names, balances, types)
  - Transactions (amounts, categories, dates, notes)
  - Budgets and budget settings
  - Financial goals
  - Subscription details
  - Categories and preferences
- **App Settings**: Theme preferences, default currency, notification settings

### How We Store Your Data

**All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database.**

- We do NOT send your financial data to any remote servers
- We do NOT store your data in the cloud
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- We do NOT track your usage or behavior
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it

## Data Usage

We use the data you provide solely to:

- Display your financial information within the app
- Calculate budgets, goals progress, and analytics
- Provide transaction history and reports
- Enable app functionality as described in the app

## Data Security

- All data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on your device
- The app uses device-level security measures provided by iOS/Android
- We recommend enabling device password/biometric authentication
- Regular security updates are provided through app updates

## Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

### Access

- View all your data within the app at any time

### Export

- Export your data in JSON format through the Settings menu
- Exported files can be saved to your device or shared as you choose

### Deletion

- Delete individual transactions, accounts, goals, or budgets
- Delete all data at once using "Delete All Data" in Settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently

### Portability

- Export your data to move it to another device
- Import previously exported data

## Third-Party Services

Cents and Sense does NOT use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- External APIs for data synchronization

## Children's Privacy

Cents and Sense does not knowingly collect or solicit data from anyone under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice within the app
- Requiring acceptance of new terms on first launch after update

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you explicitly delete it
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data
- We do not retain any backup copies of your data

## International Users

Since all data is local to your device, no international data transfers occur. However, currency support includes 150+ international currencies for your convenience.
